# 🏋️ FitTrack - Weight Loss Management App

FitTrack is a comprehensive weight loss management application designed to help users systematically achieve their fitness goals through structured training batches, daily progress logging, and habit building.

## What This Project Does

### For Users (Athletes)
*   **Structured Batches**: Access targeted weight loss programs (e.g., 21-day or 3-month courses) unlocked via payment or unique tokens.
*   **Daily Metric Tracking**: Log body weight and measurements (Face, Neck, Chest, Arms, Belly, Hips, Thighs) to monitor body recomposition.
*   **Visual Logs**: Upload left, right, and center progress photos.
*   **Meal Tracker**: Log breakfast, lunch, and dinner with photo uploads to hold yourself accountable.
*   **Streak Challenges**: Build habits with consecutive logging streaks and challenge completions.
*   **Resource Access**: Read, view, or download diet plans, exercise sheets, and coaching materials.

### For Admins (Coaches)
*   **Batch & Program Management**: Create, update, and manage workout programs.
*   **Active Monitor**: Monitor all enrolled users in real-time, view their uploaded pictures and measurements, and track compliance.
*   **Inactivity Alerts**: Receive email alerts when users fail to log their daily measurements.
*   **Direct Communication**: Connect and coach students directly.
